About Us

DEDICATION – DISCIPLINE – SINCERITY defines Gujjadi Swarna Jewellers.

We are trusted and known for quality. With this, we have built a long-term relationship with our customers.

The groundwork for this esteemed organization was laid down by Late. Gujjadi Narasimha Nayak.

A man well known for his principles – Dedication, Sincerity & Hard-work, he began his jewellery career in front-end sales. He opened his own jewellery business in 1964 in Badagupet, Udupi under the name “Gujjadi Nayak & Son’s”.

His values continue through generations, building prosperity and the unbreakable trust of our customers. Today, Gujjadi Swarna Jewellers has expanded across Karnataka with 8 branches in 6 districts, over 350 staff and artisans, and headquarters in Udupi.

Our designs blend Indian traditional art with Western elegance, crafted in-house by skilled artisans. We offer a wide variety of ornaments — from modern to classic, Indian to Western — with custom-made options to match your taste.

Our manufacturing unit in Kelarkalbettu-Udupi is best known for Temple Artifacts & Traditional Jewellery.

Every stage of our production process undergoes strict quality checks to ensure only the best gold quality reaches our customers.

For us, profit is not the only goal — our core ethics are: TRADITION, FAITH, VALUE, MODERN.

Gujjadi Swarna Jewellers became a registered company in 2011.

For over 50 years, Gujjadi Swarna has set the standard for Fine Personalized Jewellery — and that tradition continues today.
